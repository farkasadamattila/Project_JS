Z9 started as a high school project, the goal of which was to push the creative boundaries of web design. The name symbolizes the team's aspiration: to create a unique, modern platform that combines playfulness and innovation. The basic principle of the yet captivating design is that it is possible to create nostalgic and fresh experiences in the online space as well. Z9 has become a timeless testament to the power of creativity and collaboration.

Hangman

Rock-Paper-Scissors

A simple hand-gesture game where players choose rock, paper, or scissors, aiming to outsmart their opponent in this quick-thinking battle of strategy.

Snake

Don't bump into the wall

A retro arcade game where players guide a growing snake to collect items while avoiding collisions with its own tail or the edges of the screen.

Wordle

Big word knowledge needed

A daily word-guessing game where players have six tries to uncover a five-letter word, using feedback on letter placement to refine their guesses.

Tetris

Look out, it gets faster overtime

A timeless puzzle game where players arrange falling blocks to clear horizontal lines. The challenge intensifies as the speed increases. Don't let the blocks get to the top

Scrambled Words
